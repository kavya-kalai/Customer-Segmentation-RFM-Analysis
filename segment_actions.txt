Project: Task 15 - Customer Segmentation (E-commerce)
Date: February 16, 2026

1. SEGMENT: CHAMPIONS (Score: 555)
These are your best customers who bought recently, buy often, and spend the most.
- Action 1: Enroll them in a VIP Loyalty Program with exclusive early access to new product launches.
- Action 2: Send personalized 'Thank You' notes or small surprise gifts with their next order to increase brand affinity.
- Action 3: Ask for reviews or referrals, as they are the most likely to be brand advocates.

2. SEGMENT: LOYAL CUSTOMERS (High Frequency & Monetary)
These customers buy regularly and contribute significantly to revenue.
- Action 1: Use 'Upselling' strategiesâ€”recommend higher-value versions of products they already buy.

- Action 3: Provide a dedicated customer support channel to ensure they remain satisfied.

3. SEGMENT: AT RISK / HIBERNATING (Low Recency & Frequency)
These customers haven't made a purchase in a long time and are likely to stop using the platform.
- Action 1: Send a 'We Miss You' email with a high-value discount code (e.g., 20-30% off) to encourage a return.
- Action 2: Launch a 'Win-back' survey to understand why they stopped purchasing (price, quality, or service issues).
- Action 3: Show them personalized 'New Arrivals' based on their past purchase history to reignite interest.
